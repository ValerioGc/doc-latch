Read and protect your PDFs, entirely on your computer

What you can do

Key features

Multi-document tabs

Open several PDFs at once in separate tabs, each keeping its own current page, zoom level and password.

Split view

Show two open documents side by side in the same window to compare them directly, without switching tabs.

AES-256 password protection

Add or remove AES-256 encryption from your documents in a couple of clicks, right from the Protection menu. The original file is never modified.

Smooth zoom and scrolling

Navigate pages with per-tab zoom, controllable from the header bar, the status bar, or with Ctrl +/-/0 on the keyboard and the mouse wheel.

Recent documents

Quickly find recently opened files from the home screen, and remove them from the list whenever you no longer need them.

Fully offline

DocLatch requires no internet connection for any feature: opening, zooming, navigating and password-protecting documents all happen locally, with no data sent anywhere.

Light and dark theme

Switch between light and dark themes at any time from the settings. Three interface text sizes are also available.

Multilingual interface

Available in Italian, English, French and German, with the language switchable from the settings.

Compatibility

Supported formats

PDF

DocLatch currently handles PDF files only. Exporting to other formats is on the project's future roadmap.

Privacy & security

No internet. No telemetry. Ever.

DocLatch never uploads your files to any server, never sends usage data, never collects statistics, and requires no internet connection for any feature. Opening, browsing, zooming and password-protecting documents all happen entirely on your own machine.

Windows SmartScreen notice

The Windows installer isn't signed with a paid certificate yet, so it's normal for SmartScreen to show a warning on first launch. You can proceed by choosing "More info" and then "Run anyway".
